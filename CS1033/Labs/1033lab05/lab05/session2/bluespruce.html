Blue Spruce Reading Program

 

The Blue Spruce is a reading program for grades SK-3.   It is a selection of ten picture books all by Canadian authors.  The following books were chosen for the Blue Spruce Reading Program for 2002:

 

  1. The Frog Princess: by Allchin, Rosalind.  Kids Can Press $15.95

    • Best Figure Skater in the World: by Bailey, Linda, Kids Can Press $15.95

    • Omas’ Quilt: by Bourgeois, Paulette, Kids Can Press $15.95

    • Drop of Gold: by Van Klampen, Vlasta, Annick Press $18.95

  2. Stella, Queen of the Snow: by Gay, Marie-Louise. Groundwood $18.95

  3. Two So Small: by Hutchins, Hazel, Annick Press $8.95

  4. It’s Raining It’s Pouring: by Spalding, Andrea, Orca $19.95
