2. Sunquest Resorts
Hawaii
Tour Company - Sunquest Vacations

It's probably one of the most fabulous resorts anywhere! This exceptional resort has magnificent villa-type accommodations set amidst gorgeous tropical gardens, with beautiful walkways throughout to the many restaurants set all over the hotel including Mexican, Chinese , Japanese and Italian. You won't run out of dinner choices here and you can run or walk those calories off on the many pathways throughout this extensive property. Here you'll find an incredible combination of beach holiday and golf vacation, the new 18-hole Melia Cocotal Golf & Country Club is located on the premises. The beach stretches for miles and the pools here are the largest in the country!
